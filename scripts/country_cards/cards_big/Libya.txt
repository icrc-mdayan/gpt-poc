Health [ edit ] Main article: Health in Libya In 2010, spending on healthcare accounted for 3.88% of the country's GDP. In 2009, there were 18.71 physicians and 66.95 nurses per 10,000 inhabitants.  The life expectancy at birth was 74.95 years in 2011, or 72.44 years for males and 77.59 years for females.
