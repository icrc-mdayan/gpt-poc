Health [ edit ] Main article: Health in Sudan Sudan has a life expectancy of 65.1 years according to the latest data for the year 2019 from macrotrends.net  Infant mortality in 2016 was 44.8 per 1,000.  UNICEF estimates that 87% of Sudanese females between the ages of 15 and 49 have had female genital mutilation performed on them.
