Health Information in Zambia
HIV/AIDS:

Maternal and Child Mortality:
Maternal mortality rate in 2014 was 398 per 100,000 live births, improved from 591 in 2007.
Mortality rate of children under five dropped from 119 to 75 per 1,000 live births over the same period.
Despite significant improvements in health outcomes such as reduced HIV prevalence, maternal mortality, and under-five mortality, Zambia continues to face substantial health challenges.
