Health [ edit ] Main article: Health in Tunisia In 2010, spending on healthcare accounted for 3.37% of the country's GDP. In 2009, there were 12.02 physicians and 33.12 nurses per 10,000 inhabitants.  The life expectancy at birth was 75.73 years in 2016, or 73.72 years for males and 77.78 years for females.  Infant mortality in 2016 was 11.7 per 1,000.
